How to Use This Dapp

Welcome! Follow these simple steps to start using our dApp and unlock its potential:

Step 1: Install MetaMask

  • If you don’t already have MetaMask installed, visit the official MetaMask website(https://metamask.io) and download it for your browser or mobile device.
  • Set up your wallet by creating a strong password and securely storing your recovery phrase.

    • Step 2: Add Sepolia Test Network

  • Open MetaMask and click on the network dropdown at the top.
  • If the Sepolia Test Network isn’t listed, enable test networks:
    Settings > Advanced Settings > Show Test Networks (toggle it ON).
  • Choose Test Network > Sepolia.

    • Step 3: Fund Your Wallet with Sepolia ETH

    To interact with our dApp, you’ll need Sepolia ETH. Get it for free from a faucet:

  • Google Sepolia ETH faucet

    • Step 4: All done!!

  • You are now ready to use this Dapp
